Overview

The Brahma Valley trek offers a variety of sights from deeply shaded oak and rhododendron forests to snowy meadows, lakeside camping in Bekaltal, the alpine surroundings of Brahma Valley Lake, and an atmospheric view of fabulous high peaks set in a clear sky glitter. The Brahma Valley Trek is suitable for beginners and less experienced trekkers. Most of the hike is under the forest canopy, reducing the risk of altitude sickness.

Best Time to Visit: Brahmatal Trek is best for all the year to visit. Best seasons include December to March, Expect hiking on the snow in the mid of December. During winters, Brahmatal covered in the thick blanket of snow ideal for skiers. Best time to experience Brahmatal Trek in summer includes April to June.

Itinerary

Day 01: Dehradun to Lohajung

Drive:- 296 km ; Stay:- Lodge The pickup place is Dehradun railway station early in the morning. The journey uphill from Dehradun to Lohajung will take you cruising through mountain alleyways connecting the lower Himalayan belt of Garhwal hills to that of Garhwal. This is going to be a 10-hour drive, despite the marvelous changing vistas of the mountains. For the first few hours, the course of our journey will be running parallel to river Ganga and Alaknanda, flowing softly. Soon, a blustery Pindar river will meet our path. Pick-up time 06:00 am at the Dehradun railway station. The exact time you will be updated by our Trek Leader. Route:- Dehradun – Rishikesh – Devprayag – Srinagar:- Rudraprayag – Karnaprayag – Lohajung.

Day 02: Lohajung to Bekaltal

Trek:- 6 km, Stay:- Camps We will start the trek after breakfast. The first day’s trek spanning 5 hours will kick off from Lohajung up a snaky trail going into the forest. After a short while the forest will give way to a small village named Mandoli to your left. Take approximately an hour’s hike after crossing the last stream to reach the beautiful Bekaltal gleaming peacefully in the summer while partially frozen over in the winters.

Day 03: Bekaltal to Brahmatal

Trek:- 7 km, Stay:- Camps Today’s trek will take you through thickets of oaks and rhododendrons over steep slopes for quite some time before finally coming out to a meadow. This is the meadow of Telandi. In the winter, you will find the green expanse of Telandi transform into a vast snowfield and crossing the area, a gala of thrill.

Day 04: Brahmatal – Brahmatal Pass – Tilbudi

Trek:- 7 km, Stay:- Camps Start your day early in the morning as you will be heading towards the Brahmatal lake which is an uphill climb. You will be greeted with Oak and Rhododendron trees on the way which have a charm of their own. After a while, the zigzag ascent trail will take you Brahmatal Lake. After walking for a while and crossing the meadow, the final climb towards the pass begins.

Day 05: Tilbudi – Wan – Lohajung

Trek:- 4 km, Drive:- 25 km After having your breakfast you will start your descent to Wan which is a trail filled with Oak and Rhododendron trees. This is a very beautiful trail that allows you to click the end number of scenic pictures. After walking some distance you will come across a water source at Chota Tilbundi. Half an hour later you will be able to spot Wan village which is visible below. From Wan, we will take transport to reach Lohajung. Night stay in Lohajung..

Day 06: Lohajung to Dehradun

Drive :-296 kms Departure from Lohajung after breakfast. We will reach Dehradun in the evening. Trekking services will end here.
