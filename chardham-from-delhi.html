Overview

Hindus believe that completing the Char Dham Yatra circuit—visiting holy Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath and Badrinath,—in Uttarakhand’s Himalayas—cleanses sins. Follow the pilgrimage trail with ease on this private 11-day tour from Delhi, which includes transport, accommodation, and most meals. Visit Haridwar , branch out to the four sites, trek or take pony or helicopter rides (both extra cost) to the shrines; and absorb the glorious Himalayan scenery on route.

  • Follow the Char Dham Yatra Hindu trail on a private 11-day tour from Delhi
  • Hassle-free way to complete one of the holiest Hindu pilgrim circuits
  • Visit Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath; and other sacred sites
  • Private transport, camp or hotel accommodation, and most meals included
11 Days / 10 Nights

After some freshen up, then start your tour visit Mansha Devi Temple, Later in evening visit HAR- Ki-PAURI which is a famous Ghat on the bank of the Ganges in Haridwar. In the evening at sunset, the Priests of Hari Ki Pauri perform the Ganga Aarti. People flick Diya ( Made of Leaves and Flower) into the river as a symbol of Hopes and wishes. Later back to Hotel.
