Overview

Embark on a spiritual journey through the sacred sites of Kedarnath, Badrinath, and Tungnath. Begin at Kedarnath, nestled amidst the Himalayas, where the revered Kedarnath Temple stands as a symbol of devotion. Continue to Badrinath, home to the renowned Badrinath Temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Experience tranquility amidst the majestic peaks and pristine surroundings. Conclude your pilgrimage at Tungnath, the highest Shiva temple in the world, offering breathtaking views and serene ambiance. Immerse yourself in the spiritual aura, architectural marvels, and natural beauty of these revered destinations, leaving with a sense of profound peace and enlightenment.

06 Days / 05 Nights

Itinerary

Day-1 : HARIDWAR - GUPTKASHI - SITAPUR

  • Reach Haridwar.
  • A Drive Through the Mountains, Visit Devprayag (Alaknanda Bhagirathi Sangam) and Rudraprayag (Mandakini Alaknanda Sangam) on the way.
  • Reach Sitapur / Guptkashi / Sonprayag in the Evening. Check-in to the stay.
  • Explore Places on your own.
  • Overnight Stay.

DAY 2 : GUPTKASHI / SITAPUR - KEDARNATH

  • After Breakfast Check-out from the Stay. And Start our Day.
  • Drive from Sitapur / Guptkashi to Sonprayag.
  • Move to Gaurikund (Local Taxi - Self Paid Both Sides) Start the Trek to Kedarnath with Holy Dips in Gaurikund.
  • Reach Kedarnath Temple till evening, Attend Evening Aarti at Kedarnath Ji .
  • Night stay in Kedarnath.

DAY 3 : KEDARNATH TO SITAPUR GUPTAKASHI

  • After Breakfast ,Check-out from the Stay.
  • Darshan of Kedarnath Ji.
  • 22 km Down Trek at Gaurikund.
  • Night stay in Guptakashi / Sitapur.

DAY 4 : GUPTAKASHI / SITAPUR TO CHOPTA PIPALKOTI

  • After Breakfast Check Out from the Stay.
  • Get Reach For an adventures trek Towards chopta Trek by Tungnath 2.5 Km/ 1.30 hour one side.
  • Back To Chopta Sem day One drive by Pipalkoti.
  • overnight Stay Pipalkoti.

DAY 5 : PIPALKOTI TO BADRINATH DARSHAN BACK TO PIPALKOTI / RUDRPARYAG

  • Have breakfast.
  • Proceed to Badrinath. (75 km/2 Hours ).
  • On the way will do some sightseeing Reach badrinath Dham.
  • Attend Darshan in Badrinath Dham Overnight Stay at Pipalkoti / Srinagar.
  • Mana Village.

DAY 6 : RUDRAPRAYAG TO HARIDWAR/ RISHIKESH

  • After Breakfast Check-out from the Stay.
  • Get ready for a beautiful drive in the DevBhoomi Uttrakhand.
  • Reach Rishikesh / Haridwar in the Evening.
  • Time to end the trip with lots of memories and Emotions towards Kedarnath & Badrinath Dham.
  • Attend by Ganga Aarti Haridwar/ Rishikesh.
