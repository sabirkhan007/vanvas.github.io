Overview

Bali pass is such a popular winter trek that it would be apt to entitle it as the Queen of Winter Treks. Knee-deep snow in winters, mesmerizing view from the top, and the beautiful and easy trails make it popular among trekkers. The ‘easy on endurance’ trail goes through the thick pine forests of Govind National Park. The views from the top are worth climbing to an altitude of 12,500 ft. CharDham Treks will give you an inimitable view of the cosmic sky, lush meadows, snow paths, charming villages, scented pine forest, sky-knocking peaks, tranquil rivers, and some mythological story whispers.

Best Time to Visit: Bali Pass Trek is Beautiful and Pleasant for whole Year. Best time of year include December to April, Expect trekking on the snow in the mid of December. During winters, Bali pass covered in the thick blanket of snow best for skiers.

Itinerary

Day 01:Haridwar to Janki Chatti

Arrive At Haridwar Start Your Tour towards Janki Chatti. ( 219 km approx | 6-7 hrs Drive). Once you reached there, check into the pre-booked hotel. Take rest overnight in the hotel.

Day 02: Janki Chatti - Yamunotri - Janki Chatti

On the next day morning post breakfast, start moving towards Yamunotri. Start 6 km trekking to reach Yamunotri. You can also choose horse ride or ponis for your own preference to reach Yamunotri without walking too much. Yamunotri is the shrine of goddess Yamuna. Here you can enjoy the darshan of goddess and get blessing of maa. Here you will explore Yamunotri temple, Divya Shila, Surya Kund and many other places in and around the temple. Take the cooked rice Prasad and return back to Jankichatti before evening. Take rest overnight in the hotel..

Day 03: Janki Chati to Uttarkashi

On the 3rd day morning post breakfast, start moving towards Uttarkashi. Uttarkashi is situated 124 km away from Janki Chatti which will take 4-5 hr by road to reach. Once you reached Uttarkashi, check into the pre-booked hotel. After freshen up in the hotel, visit the famous Vishwanath temple for getting the blessing of god. Later, return to hotel for night stay.

Day 04: Uttarkashi - Gangotri - Uttarkashi

On the next day morning post breakfast, start moving towards Gangotri from Uttarkashi. Gangotri is situated 100 km away from Uttarkashi which will take 2-3 hr by road to reach. En-route you will witness the majestic Himalayan mountain range, Harsil village and the beautiful Bhagirathi River. Later you will reach the Gangotri Temple. Gangotri temple is the abode of goddess Ganga. Here you will witness Gangotri temple, surya kund, Bhagirath Shila etc. Return to Uttarkashi during evening. Take rest in the hotel overnight.

Day 05: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi SITAPUR

On the 5th day of your trip after enjoying delicious breakfast in the hotel, start touring to Guptkashi/ Sitapur Guptkashi is situated 197km away from Uttarkashi which will take 7- 8hr by road to reach. While driving towards Guptkashi / Sitapur you will see the captivating Mandakini River at Tilwara. After reaching Guptkashi / Sitapur, check into the pre-booked hotel. Overnight Stay in Hotel.

Day 06: Guptkashi / Sitapur To Kedarnath dham

After Breakfast Check-out from the Stay. And Start our Day. Drive from Sitapur / Guptkashi to Sonprayag. Move to Gaurikund (Local Taxi - Self Paid Both Sides). Start the Trek to Kedarnath with Holy Dips in Gaurikund. Reach Kedarnath Temple till evening, Attend Evening Aarti at Kedarnath Ji. Night stay in Kedarnath..

Day 07: Kedarnath dham to Chopta to Sitapur

After Breakfast , Check-out from the Stay. Do Darshan of Kedarnath Ji. 19 km Down Trek & 74 km/ 3 hr by Road). Get ready for a Adventurous Trek Towards Chopta. Chopta is a small region of meadows and evergreen forest area, a part of Kedarnath wildlife sanctuary located in Uttarakhand state, India. Have Dinner. Overnight Stay In Sitapur.

Day 08: Sitapur-Badrinath dham

Have breakfast. Proceed to Badrinath. (175 km/5-6hr). On the way will do some sightseeing. Reach badrinath Dham. Attend aarti in Badrinath Dham. Overnight Stay at Govindghat / Badrinath.

Day 09: BADRINATH– Rishikesh / HARIDWAR

After Breakfast Check-out from the Stay. Get ready for a beautiful drive in the Dev Bhoomi Uttrakhand. Reach Rishikesh / Haridwar in the Evening. Time to end the trip with lots of memories and Emotions towards Kedarnath & Badrinath Dham..
