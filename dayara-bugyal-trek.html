Overview

During the Dayara Bugyal Trek, you will come across lush green prairies or “ Bugyals” spread across the Himalayas, which are a sight to behold. Out of these, the Dayara Bugyal is especially magnicent, standing tall at a height of 12,000 ft. It is like a tabletop, spread over 28 sq. km. The meadow presents a breathtaking vista both in the winter season - with its promise of an endless expanse of glittering snow, making it a famous winter trek - and in the summer season, with the sunlight reecting dierent shades of green as far as the eye can see, and yellow, violet and white owers bursting in places. The Dayara Bugyal Trek maps the distance from Haridwar to the Himalayas.

The base camp for the Dayara Bugyal trek is at Barsu, perched at an altitude of 7,545 ft, this quaint little hamlet overlooks the green slopes of the valley on one side, and the snowy peaks of the Himalayas on the other. It is located at a distance of about 32 km from Uttarkashi. The trek will take you through dense forests of towering Oak and Pine trees, along the Bhagirathi river, across lush green meadows, and through bubbling streams and rivulets. The Dayara Bugyal Trek is a trek of a relatively easy level of diculty and can be undertaken even by beginners and rst-timers!

04 Days / 03 Nights

Itinerary

Day 1 Dehradun to Natin (8530 ft) (200 km Drive)
  • Pick up from Dehradun Railway station at 7.30.a.m.
  • Drive to Raithal via Uttarkashi
  • Arrive at Natin village, a small village located 30 km above the city of Uttarkashi which serves as the starting point of the trek. Stay at the homestay in Natin.
  • A quick introduction & brieng session with the trek leader and fellow travelers .
  • Dinner and rest at the homestay.
Day 2 : Natin to Gui( 9500 ft) (5 km, 6-7 hours trek)
  • The day will start with a group ice-breaking session and Breakfast will be served.
  • Afterward, we'll take an uphill short trek of 5 KM through the Oak Forest in order to reach Campsite.
  • On arrival at Gui Campsite, Lunch will be served.
  • The lunch will be followed by an acclimatization walk and evening snacks will be served.
  • Overnight stay at camps.
Day 3 : Gui to Dayara Bugyal Top (12100 Ft) back to Gui (12 Km)
  • Post breakfast, we'll head out for trekking to Dayara Bugyal.
  • On arrival, we'll have a packed lunch at Dayara viewpoint.
  • After sightseeing, we'll descend back to the Gui campsite.
  • On arrival at Gui Campsite, we'll have dinner and end the day.
Day 4 : Gui to Dehradun | Descend Back to Dehradun
  • Gui to Raithal, Drive to Dehradun, 235 Km Drive.
  • Descend back to Raithal village from Gui after breakfast on a 5 km descend the trail
  • A 235 km drive back to Dehradun from Raithal village passing through Uttarkashi,
  • Lunch and evening snacks during transport will be provided on the roadside inns.
  • The expected time of arrival in Dehradun will be 7:30 PM to 8:00 PM . So you can plan your travel further accordingly.
Reporting Time
