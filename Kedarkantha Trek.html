Overview

Kedarkantha is such a popular winter trek that it would be apt to entitle it as the Queen of Winter Treks. Knee-deep snow in winters, mesmerizing view from the top, and the beautiful and easy trails make it popular among trekkers. The ‘easy on endurance’ trail goes through the thick pine forests of Govind National Park. The views from the top are worth climbing to an altitude of 12,500 ft. Kedarkantha trek will give you an inimitable view of the cosmic sky, lush meadows, snow paths, charming villages, scented pine forest, sky-knocking peaks, tranquil rivers, and some mythological story whispers.

Best Time to Visit: Kedarkantha Trek is Beautiful and Pleasant for whole Year. Best time of year include December to April, Expect trekking on the snow in the mid of December. During winters, Kedarkantha covered in the thick blanket of snow best for skiers.

Itinerary

Day 01: From Dehradun to Sankri drive

The congregation is set to occur in Dehradun, serving as the initial point of departure. Your journey will commence as you are transported to the base camp, the starting point for the upcoming trek. While the entire voyage from Dehradun to Sankri holds its own allure, a particularly noteworthy aspect is the sight of majestic pine trees that greet you after the passage through Purola. The most enchanting stretch, however, unfolds as you traverse the Mori valley alongside the Tonnes River.

The duration of travel between Dehradun and Sankri spans approximately 10 hours, covering a distance of 220 kilometers. Sankri, positioned at an elevation of 2,000 meters (6,400 feet), boasts limited network connectivity due to its high-altitude location.

Day 02: Trek from Sankri to Juda Ka Talab

Following an overnight stay in Sankri, our journey progresses towards the next destination—Juda Ka Talab. This enchanting location spans an elevation ranging from 6,400 feet to 9,100 feet. The hike spans approximately four hours and guides travelers through dense pine forests, groves of maple trees, and numerous babbling streams. Along this picturesque trail, the captivating Juda Ka Talab awaits.

The legends surrounding Juda Ka Talab are diverse, each weaving its own unique narrative. One such tale recounts that Lord Shiva, in ancient times, sought solace at this very site for his meditation. Overcome by thirst, he is said to have plucked a single strand of hair from his mane and cast it upon the earth. This simple gesture is said to have given rise to the tranquil pool of water that now graces this location. Another local version of the story narrates the existence of two distinct water bodies that eventually merged into the unified expanse known as Juda Ka Talab.

Day 03: Trek from Juda Ka Talab to Kedarkantha Base Camp

In this leg of the trek, we will cover a total distance of 4 kilometers while ascending from an altitude of 9,100 feet to 11,250 feet. During this phase, participants will be treated to panoramic views of renowned mountains such as Bandarpoonch, Swargarohini, Kala Naag, and Ranglana. These mountains carry a plethora of myths and tales, yet as the adage goes, "Find contentment in the experience, not in the narrative," as myths and legends can evolve. Nevertheless, the imprints of an experience remain unparalleled.

Nightfall will find us stationed at the Kedarkantha Base Camp. The subsequent day will see us embarking towards the summit, ready to conquer the next leg of the journey.

Day 04: Trek base camp to Kedarkantha summit

The stunning Himalayan vistas that unfold at the culmination of the battle serve as ample recompense for the exertions invested. Moreover, nestled in a picturesque expanse, stands a modest temple dedicated to the deities Shiva, Parvati, and Ganesha. This locale is recognized as a sanctum, with a legend narrating Lord Shiva's bestowal of his blessings upon it. This tale stems from the annals of mythology. Astonishingly, an extraordinary element about this spot awaits your discovery—a connection that draws you back repeatedly, compelling you to return as often as possible. Here, avenues to receive divine favor lie open.

Search for the sanctuary that resonates with your inner tranquility, fortifying you for the rigors of daily life. When time permits, consider indulging in moments of meditation. This proves to be the most efficacious approach to unwind and revitalize following a whirlwind journey. Following a sojourn in this haven, our descent shall eventually commence, guiding us back to the Kedarkantha Base camp.

Day 05: From Kedarkantha base camp to Sankri

As we approach the culmination of the Kedarkantha trek, we will reach our destination, marking our final evening together, cherishing the collective experience. The descent back to Sankri holds an equal measure of excitement, mirroring the thrill of our ascent to the summit.

Day 06: Drive from Sankri to Dehradun

Our arrival at the destination aligns with the culmination of the Kedarkantha trek, marking the final day of our shared expedition. This evening holds a special significance as it's the last night we'll spend cherishing this collective journey. The descent from the mountain's summit to Sankri is equally thrilling and invigorating, mirroring the excitement that accompanied our ascent to the peak initially.
