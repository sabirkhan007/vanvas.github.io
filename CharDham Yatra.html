    Trek Itinerary

    01

    Day 1: Haridwar to Janki Chatti

    • Arrive At Haridwar
    • Start Your Tour towards Janki Chatti.
    • ( 219 km approx | 6-7 hrs Drive).
    • Once you reached there, check into the pre-booked hotel. Take rest overnight in the hotel.

    02

    Day 2: Janki Chatti - Yamunotri - Janki Chatti

    • On the next day morning post breakfast, start moving towards Yamunotri.
    • Start 6 km trekking to reach Yamunotri.
    • You can also choose horse ride or ponis for your own preference to reach Yamunotri without walking too much. Yamunotri is the shrine of goddess Yamuna.
    • Here you can enjoy the darshan of goddess and get blessing of maa. Here you will explore Yamunotri temple, Divya Shila, Surya Kund and many other places in and around the temple.
    • Take the cooked rice Prasad and return back to Jankichatti before evening.
    • Take rest overnight in the hotel.

    03

    Day 3: Janki Chati to Uttarkashi

    • On the 3rd day morning post breakfast, start moving towards Uttarkashi.
    • Uttarkashi is situated 124 km away from Janki Chatti which will take 4-5 hr by road to reach.
    • Once you reached Uttarkashi, check into the pre-booked hotel. After freshen up in the hotel, visit the famous Vishwanath temple for getting the blessing of god.
    • Later, return to hotel for night stay.

    04

    Day 4: Uttarkashi - Gangotri - Uttarkashi

    • On the next day morning post breakfast, start moving towards Gangotri from Uttarkashi.
    • Gangotri is situated 100 km away from Uttarkashi which will take 2-3 hr by road to reach.
    • En-route you will witness the majestic Himalayan mountain range, Harsil village and the beautiful Bhagirathi River.
    • Later you will reach the Gangotri Temple. Gangotri temple is the abode of goddess Ganga.
    • Here you will witness Gangotri temple, surya kund, Bhagirath Shila etc. Return to Uttarkashi during evening.
    • Take rest in the hotel overnight.

    05

    Day 5: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi SITAPUR

    • On the 5th day of your trip after enjoying delicious breakfast in the hotel, start touring to Guptkashi/ Sitapur
    • Guptkashi is situated 197km away from Uttarkashi which will take 7- 8hr by road to reach.
    • While driving towards Guptkashi / Sitapur you will see the captivating Mandakini River at Tilwara.
    • After reaching Guptkashi / Sitapur, check into the pre-booked hotel.
    • Overnight Stay in Hotel.

    06

    Day 6: Guptkashi / Sitapur To Kedarnath dham

    • After Breakfast Check-out from the Stay. And Start our Day.
    • Drive from Sitapur / Guptkashi to Sonprayag.
    • Move to Gaurikund (Local Taxi - Self Paid Both Sides).
    • Start the Trek to Kedarnath with Holy Dips in Gaurikund.
    • Reach Kedarnath Temple till evening, Attend Evening Aarti at Kedarnath Ji.
    • Night stay in Kedarnath.

    07

    Day 7: Kedarnath dham to Chopta to Sitapur

    • After Breakfast , Check-out from the Stay.
    • Do Darshan of Kedarnath Ji.
    • 19 km Down Trek & 74 km/ 3 hr by Road).
    • Get ready for a Adventurous Trek Towards Chopta.
    • Chopta is a small region of meadows and evergreen forest area, a part of Kedarnath wildlife sanctuary located in Uttarakhand state, India.
    • Have Dinner.
    • Overnight Stay In Sitapur.

    08

    Day 8: Sitapur-Badrinath dham

    • Have breakfast.
    • Proceed to Badrinath. (175 km/5-6hr).
    • On the way will do some sightseeing.
    • Reach badrinath Dham.
    • Attend aarti in Badrinath Dham.
    • Overnight Stay at Govindghat / Badrinath.

    09

    Day 9: BADRINATHâ€“ Rishikesh / HARIDWAR

    • After Breakfast Check-out from the Stay.
    • Get ready for a beautiful drive in the Dev Bhoomi Uttrakhand.
    • Reach Rishikesh / Haridwar in the Evening.
    • Time to end the trip with lots of memories and Emotions towards Kedarnath & Badrinath Dham.
